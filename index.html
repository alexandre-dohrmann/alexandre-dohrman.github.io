About Me

After spending 13-years in the AdTech industry in various capacities (Planning, Account Mangement, Project Management, Sales, and People/Processes Management), I found myself yearning for more. I knew that I wanted a career that satisfied my desire for constant learning but was also equal-parts creative and more logical/mathematical. I had always wanted to learn to code and after doing a lot of research, found that web development had all of the components that I was looking for in a career switch. I have truly found my calling in web development and am always looking for new side projects and learning opportunities.
